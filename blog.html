26/10/2016

XConnect MVP Update

By Eric N.

Hello and Welcome to the first post of our blog

We are pleased to inform we are working on Release 2 of our Single Sign On MVP (Minimum Viable Product), that will integrate SMS OTP as a second factor for authentication.

For Authentication Providers:

Because of its security enhancement, This feature eases the adoption of our Single Sign On technology by client applications and therefore foster broader eploitation of your user base information.

For Client Applications:

In addition to relying on an existing authentication infrastructure, you can ensure that users are authenticated according to your desired level of security (login/password, SMS OTP, login/password+SMS OTP, ....).

For the sake of customer experience, a second factor would be typically required only the first time the user authenticates to the applicaitonusing X Connect. However, this policy is at the discretion of the Client application.

Notice

Release 3 of the MVP will include Mobile Connect, a GSM standaridized technology for two factor authentication and athorization solely based on the mobile number and a permanent secret code created by the user on the mobile device SIM card.
