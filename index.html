XConnect

Garanteeing user identity by relying on their account at third party providers

What's X Connect?

X Connect is a service that avoid the hassle of handling connection credentials for each and every web application where a user has to connect.


X Connect allows a user to open a session at a web application by using their credentials at a third party provider, that authenticates the user on behalf of the web application.


We deliver the solution that fits your organization and address uour burdens.

How does it work?

Look at the following kinematic to understand how X Connect facilitates the life of your users.





Step 1


The user wants to connect to their web or mobile application but they don't want to create login/password



Step 2


They opt for X Connect Log-in and connect with their credentials at their favorite provider or connect using SMS One Time Password



Step 3


They are logged in their application and their user profile is automatically stored in their account..
