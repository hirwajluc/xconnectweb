Demonstrations

Note that all the web applications and accounts are demo applications and demo accounts.

Scenario 1

Tom is a rwandese citizen at Butare. He often uses Irembo for his administrative procedures. Tom wants to apply for a scolarship from the city. He does so by opening an account at the city portal. Tom already has credentials for 5 or 6 government applications and would love not to create new ones. You are Tom in this demo/


1) Sign in at Butare CityHall web portal by using on your credentials at Irembo. For the purpose of the emo, you will get an XConnect branded authentication page.


2) Apply for the scolarship. Based on your verified identity at irembo, the web application will ftech your High School diploma grade at Rwanda Education Board

Scenario 2

John is an entrepreneur who registered his company through Rwanda Development Board(RdB), the Business Gateway in Rwanda. John often authenticates at RdB for various operations on his account. John would be happy if his other business related accounts (like the one at RSSB) do not require him to have different login and password. You are John in the demo.


1) Sign in at Rwanda Social Security Board application by using your identity at RdB. For the purpose of the emo, you will get an XConnect branded authentication page.


2) Once authenticated, declare your payroll at RSSB using your TIN number (Business registration identifier in Rwanda).

Scenario 3

You are a digital addict. You spend a lot of time on the web and would like to easily connect to her applications that require login and password. You are the user in the demo.
