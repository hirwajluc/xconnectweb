  4. 4. FDS is committed to transmitting the information required by the Service Provider via the service. The categories of transmitted information and their conditions of treatment by the service provider are defined in these terms and conditions.
  5. 5. FDS is committed to checking the identity information by calling on additional services, if necessary, rectifying the identity of the user before transmission of information to the Service Provider. The responsibility of FDS could not be committed in the event of usurpation of identity or any fraudulent use of the service.
  6. 6. FDS is committed to carrying out acceptance tests before each commissioning of X Connect for the Service Providez
  7. 7. FDS is committed to making X Connect accessible at least in the same conditions as the Service Provider.
  8. 8. FDS is committed to implementing all appropriate measures, of which some are described in this terms and conditions, in order to protect the data treated within the framework of the Service.
  9. 9. FDS is responsible for the data processed within the framework of the service and as such, is committed to respecting the obligations inherent to this treatment.
  10. 10. FDS is committed to ensuring the traceability of all the actions carried out by the users of the service.
  11. 11. FDS is committed to ensuring the follow-up and the evaluation of the use of the service, and communicating the results got to the various partners

Data conservation duration

During Phase 1 of the trial, the data constituting the pivotal identity is stored in a database by X Connect. These data should not be valid and real user data.

During Phase 2 onwards, the data constituting the pivotal identity is not stored in a database by X Connect. Data is saved in server-side session for a period of 30 minutes.
Data is retrieved by X Connect at each connection of the user. X Connect retains the thirty-six month history of user's connection in a log file
There is no conservation limit of stored data base ( hash key , couples client_id / sub)

Security Considerations

X Connect obligations

X Connect implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect data processed and stored as part of the service, and in view of security goals identified following the analysis of security risks. These measures concern in particular:

  • Routine monitoring of all requests input paramters to reduce the risk injection. X Connect implements customer locking mechanisms in case of repeated connection failures, this to prevent brute force attacks. This measure may lead to permanent disconnection of the Service Provider in cases of critical threat,
  • secrets robustness, storage, secure transmission and regular renewal
  • in general: the application of defense in depth principles, particularly in management of access rights to the various components of the system (reverse proxies, servers application and data etc.).

Service Provider obligations

it belongs to the Service provider to implement technical and organizational measures that are appropriate to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, accidental loss, alteration , disclosure or unauthorized access, particularly in the context of the transmission of data through an insecure network, as well as against any other form of unlawful processing

The Service Provider shall implement the technical and organizational security measures necessary to ensure , on its perimeter :

  • The non-disclosure of the functional and technical data exchanged under the OpenID Connect Protocol to an unauthorized third party
  • implementing measures to prevent their escape in case of intrusion
  • confidentiality and integrity of traded secrets ( passwords, cryptographic keys )
